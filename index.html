Don't Let YouTube Track You

Your YouTube history, owned by you — not Google. Stored locally, encrypted, synced across devices. No Google account required.

Features

🔒

Private Local Storage

Your history is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud unless you choose to sync.

🛡️

Encrypted Cross-Device Sync

Sync your history across devices using end-to-end AES-256 encryption. Even the sync server can't read your data.

🔍

Search, Export & Import

Full-text search through your history. Export as JSON or CSV. Import it back anytime. Your data, your format.

🌍

Works Everywhere

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera, and Vivaldi. No Google account needed — pair devices with a simple 6-character code.

Privacy by Design

No analytics. No telemetry. No cloud storage by default. All sync data is end-to-end encrypted. The extension only accesses youtube.com. Your browsing history is yours alone.
